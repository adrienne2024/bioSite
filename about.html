Jessie is a woman of many accomplishments. She has tried her hand at many jobs, from working in attractions an entertainment costuming at Disneyland to various customer service related jobs. She is currently a recruiter for Blizzard International where she has worked for the last three years. Jessie is a Certified Make-Up artist and landed her first professional gig at Knott's Scary Farm right after graduation from Cinema Make-up School in Los Angeles. She is also a professionally trained vocalist and can be seen performing with her band The Merry Wives of Windsor at Renassaince Faires all over the country. She also enjoys performing with her husband Garrett at the Rose Center Theater in Westminister, CA. The two have appeared together in The Little Mermaid, Beauty and the Beast and the Easter presentation of The Last Supper.
